A LITTLE ABOUT ME

My venture towards becoming a software developer is taking a new route in web development! I’m currently attending a Bootcamp for web development through LaGuardia Community College’s Tech Hire Open Code program in partnership with General Assembly.

In 2016, I completed an Android development Bootcamp through Pursuit Core, which sparked my passion for coding. Through this program, with first engaging in understanding core Java fundamentals, many Android-based programs were built, such as a calculator, meme generator, emergency application (built-in a 48-hour hackathon), and a productivity app (published in the google play store and demonstrated at the museum of moving image).

In 2019, I completed an internship, with an amazing web-based eConsult service company called RubiconMD, where my team and I built an image labeling program in Python using the Amazon Recognition API.

I currently use my free time to volunteer at a Karate School in my community as a technical assistant. I’ve worked on several projects such as re-creating the karate school logo, an embossed logo, as well as creating flyers and videos for events and programs to increase customer engagement. I also leverage the skills obtained as a consultation technician at GeekSquad to fulfill my duties as a technical assistant.
